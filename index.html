Research Highlights

Developed a new depth-averaged numerical model capable of simulating the co-seismic evolution of submarine landslides, incorporating three-component seismic loading, strain-softening behavior, and terrain curvature effects. The proposed approach was applied to two case studies â€” large-scale submarine landslides on the southwest Iberian margin and the well-documented St. Niklausen Slide in Lake Lucerne, Switzerland. The simulated morphologies show strong agreement with field observations in both cases.
